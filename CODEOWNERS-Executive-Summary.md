## What Is CODEOWNERS?

CODEOWNERS defines who must review changes to specific parts of a codebase.

---

## Why It Matters

- Improves accountability
- Reduces operational risk
- Supports compliance and audit requirements
- Enables scalable governance

---

## How It Is Enforced

- CODEOWNERS identifies owners
- Branch protection or rulesets require approvals
- CI validates correctness

---

## Platform Considerations

- **GHEC**: Best-in-class enterprise governance via rulesets
- **GHES**: Governance depends on deployed version

---

## Recommendation

Adopt CODEOWNERS as a **mandatory governance control**
for high-risk repositories.
